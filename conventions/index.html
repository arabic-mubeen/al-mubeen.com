# Heavy letters

There are pairs of letters where one as just a heavy version of the other.

Heavy letter EN Letter EN
ح H هـ h
ق q ك k
ص S س s
ض D د d
ظ Z ذ ḏ
ط T ت t

# Tanween

Tanween is indicated by square brackets to 1) distinguish it from Alif + Nun (ان) and 2) hint out the rule of stopping.

ـً [an]
ـٍ [en]
ـٌ [un]

Example:

  1. العربية: Al3arabyyah

Check more examples in the reading practice for lesson2

# Gender and Duals

Gender is indicated by [f] for feminine nouns and adjectives, and [m] otherwise. Dual is indicated by [2].

  1. هذا: this [m]
  2. هذه: this [f]
  3. هذان: these [2]
